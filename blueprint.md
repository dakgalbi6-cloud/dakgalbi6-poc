# Speetto 1000 Scratch-off Simulator

## Overview
A realistic web-based scratch-off lottery simulator inspired by the South Korean "Speetto 1000". Users can use their mouse or touch to scratch a silver coating and reveal numbers to win prizes.

## Features
- **Realistic Scratching:** Uses HTML5 Canvas and `globalCompositeOperation` for a natural erasing effect.
- **Speetto 1000 Logic:** Matches "Lucky Numbers" with "My Numbers" to determine winners.
- **Modern Aesthetics:** Vibrant colors, multi-layered shadows, and responsive design.
- **Auto-Reveal:** Automatically reveals the entire area once 80% is scratched.

## Technical Implementation
- **HTML5 Canvas:** Handles the scratchable overlay.
- **CSS Layers:** Manages the ticket layout, backgrounds, and interactive elements.
- **JavaScript ES Modules:** Organizes the scratch engine and game logic.

## Project Structure
- `index.html`: Ticket layout and canvas container.
- `style.css`: Visual styling, gradients, and animations.
- `main.js`: Scratch-off engine and lottery logic.
